Biography

Tatiana Sendin has produced in-depth stories across urban inequality, labor transitions, public services, and civic life. She has interviewed policymakers, workers, and community organizers, and regularly collaborates with editors on narrative and investigative projects.

In addition to reporting, she mentors early-career journalists in narrative structure, sourcing, and ethics. Her editorial approach is grounded in precision, empathy, and a strong commitment to public accountability.
