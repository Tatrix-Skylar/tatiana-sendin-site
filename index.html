Biography

Tatiana Sendin is an independent journalist and editor with more than a decade of experience producing in-depth features and investigations. Her work explores where policy meets lived experience, with a particular focus on urban inequality, labor, and education.

She has contributed to regional and international publications, interviewed community leaders and public officials, and mentored young reporters in narrative and solutions journalism. Her approach combines rigorous reporting, clear language, and a deep commitment to public service.
